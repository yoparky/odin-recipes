Fresh Tomato Soup

Tomato soup, made with red tomatoes. Creamy, fresh, and ready in 35 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 1 slice onion
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablesppons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine the tomatoes, onion, cloves, and chicken broth in a stackpot over medium heat.
  2. Gently boil for about 20 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat, run mixture through a food mill into a large bowl. Discard leftover stuff in food mill.
  4. Melt butter in an empty stockpot over medium heat.
  5. Add flour to make a roux.
  6. Cook roux until it is medium brown.
  7. Slowly add a bits of the tomato mixture, ensuring no lumps are formed.
  8. Stir, season with brown sugar and salt.
